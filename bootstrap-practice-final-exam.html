How a Bill Becomes a Law

Step 1

Bill is introduced in the House of Representatives.

Step 2

Bill goes to a committee where it may be amended.

Step 3

Bill passes out of committee and is scheduled for floor debate.

Step 4

Bill goes to House or Senate floor, where it may be amended. If passed, it continues to the Governor.

Step 5

Once the House and Senate have agreed on the bill's language, the governor signs it into law or vetoes it.
